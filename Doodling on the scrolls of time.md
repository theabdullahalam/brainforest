---
title: "Doodling on the scrolls of time"
tags: ["poetry"]
layout: note.njk
date: 2026-03-03
---
All these words  
and all these feelings  
rain around me  
before draining  
into this giant  
delicate inkpot  
located somewhere  
inside my heart.

I must write,  
I must draw,  
I must doodle.  

I must doodle  
not just with words  
but by singing  
or by dancing  
or by playing  
or by doing  
just about anything  
to dip my quill  
and sip the ink  
little by little  
and let those words  
and all those feelings  
leave marks on  
the never-ending  
scrolls of time.

Or risk the inkpot  
flowing over,  
leaving stains  
on my heart  
and my mind  
and my body  
and my soul.

I must write,  
I must draw,  
I must doodle.

I must doodle  
so as to not  
stain my soul.

*- Abdullah Alam*
