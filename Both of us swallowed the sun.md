---
title: "Both of us swallowed the sun"
tags: ["poetry"]
layout: note.njk
date: 2025-05-25
---
We both  
swallowed the sun  
with beady eyed  
excitement.

Before long,  
our insides burnt  
and we  
spit it out.  

Now we  
miss the warmth  
but are  
able to breathe.

Isn't it sad  
how happy we are?

_-Abdullah Alam_
