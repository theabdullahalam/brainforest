---
title: "All You Want is A Feeling"
tags: ["poetry"]
layout: note.njk
---
How do we learn  
to love each other  
when we crave  
different things?

How can our puzzle  
pieces fit  
when my piece  
looks like  
wanting you  
and  
your piece  
looks like  
wanting a feeling?  

How do I love  
with all of my heart  
when all I want  
is you,  
and  
all you want  
is someone to love.

_-Abdullah Alam_
