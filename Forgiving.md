---
title: "Forgiving"
tags: ["poetry"]
layout: note.njk
date: 2020-11-11
---
You grew up  
not knowing  
Not every flower  
is plucked in bloom.

Never knowing love,  
You'd wilt out of fear  
from afar.

So  
stop hiding your  
secret love for gloom;

For the world  
is just as forgiving  
as you are.

_-Abdullah Alam_
