---
title: "A Walk in the Sky"
tags: ["poetry"]
layout: note.njk
date: 2021-10-03
---
Why is it that  
feelings only  
feel real  
when directed to someone?

Why does music  
make you yearn  
for having  
a lover to yearn?

Why can't love,  
like clouds,  
just exist?  

Wish we could love  
and lust  
and ache  
and envy  
as aimlessly  
as an evening walk.

Wish we could go  
for a walk in the sky.

_- Abdullah Alam_
