# Hello World
Hello World, welcome to my blog. I have maintained several blogs over the years, but all of them have been _about_ something, and not tied to my identity. Having recently re-read Austin Kleon's Steal Like An Artist, and the excellent Almanack of Naval Ravikant, I felt inspired to start a blog publicly available, tied to my identity as an individual. Consider this an experiment in digital vulnerability.

This blog has an extremely simple structure - it is a stream of posts about anything I feel will be of interest to people. In place of a fixed "tagging" system, I invite you to see the pages that page links to as a hint toward what categories that post falls under. Like an intricate connections of thoughts, clustered only by connections, and having a one-dimensional depth.
