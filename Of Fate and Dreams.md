---
title: "Of Fate and Dreams"
tags: ["poetry"]
layout: note.njk
---
When my life  
was totally different,   
I dreamt of a life  
quite different to it.  

But then my life  
took a sharp left  
and became a third  
unexpected thing.  

In love of the  
unexpected, I  
lost track of  
all of my dreams.  

Now, when the unexpected  
brings you joy,  
you forget about  
your dreams and sing  
praise for all of  
your good fortune.

But when the same  
unexpected  
brings you stress  
and sorrows instead,  
you curse it for  
holding you back  
from your dreams.   

Is it ungrateful  
to forsake the fruits  
borne by fate  
if the very same fate  
is forbidding your dreams?  

_-Abdullah Alam_
